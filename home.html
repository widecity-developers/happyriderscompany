Places We have Explored

Waynad off road ride

The Wayanad Off-Road Ride is a thrilling adventure, guiding avid bikers through the challenging terrains of Wayanad in the Western Ghats, offering a unique blend of adrenaline-fueled excitement and breathtaking natural beauty.

Vattavada off road ride

The Vattavada Off-Road Ride is an exhilarating biking experience that takes riders through the rugged and scenic landscapes of Vattavada, offering a thrilling adventure across challenging terrains surrounded by the pristine beauty of the Western Ghats.

Rider's Mania Goa

Rider's Mania Goa is an annual gathering that brings together motorcycle enthusiasts from across the country, creating a vibrant and spirited community celebration where riders can share their passion, attend workshops, participate in thrilling rides, and immerse themselves in the diverse culture of motorcycling.

Leh Ladakh Ride

The Leh Ladakh Ride is an iconic motorcycle journey that takes riders through the breathtaking landscapes of the Himalayas, offering a once-in-a-lifetime adventure with its challenging mountain passes, serene lakes, and high-altitude deserts, making it a pilgrimage for motorcycle enthusiasts seeking the ultimate two-wheeled expedition.
